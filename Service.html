My Services

I accept reviews request from authors, but my review policy will have to be followed. PLEASE READ MY REVIEW POLICY.

Review Policy

  • I accept review request from authors, but it will depend on the genre of thier books. Ther are certain genres I don't read.
  • If the offered book is part of a series which happens to be the sequel or the third book in the series -but I haven't read the series yet- please note that I will accept the request, but I might need the previous book(s) as well.
  • I can only accept E-books and Finished copies, I don't prefer physical copies since international shipping is very expensive. And I don't prefer audiobooks for various reasons.

I only accept books in following genre

  • Fantasy
    • Urban-fantasy
    • Epic-fantasy
    • Fairytale Retellings
    • Historical-fantasy
    • High-fantasy
    • Paranormal-fantasy with angels, demons, and nephilims
  • Sci-fi books will be declined
  • Paranormal-fantasy/Romance with shapeshifters and vampires will be declined
  • Books with explicit/spicy/smutty/steamy contents will be declined
  • Reverse Harem Fantasy will be declined

Accepting Request

Once I have accepted the request, I will get back to you and email you the link(s) to my review. I'm only getting back to you to inform you about my review once I finish writing it and publish the post on my blog.

My Review

All of my reviews are honest and real in a respectful way, so it can be so it can be either positive or negative or both.
